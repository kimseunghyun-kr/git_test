strawberry_pie

description

a simple strawberry pie

ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 1 quart fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¾ cup water
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream

directions

  1. Arrange half of strawberries in baked pastry shell. Mash remaining berries and

    combine with sugar in a medium saucepan. Place saucepan over medium heat and

    bring to a boil, stirring frequently.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water. Gradually stir cornstarch

    mixture into boiling strawberry mixture. Reduce heat and simmer mixture until

    thickened, about 10 minutes, stirring constantly. Pour mixture over berries in pastry shell

  3. Chill for several hours before serving. In a small bowl, whip cream until soft peaks

    form. Serve each slice of pie with a dollop of whipped cream.
